# Style Guide

## Tone
- Authentic, realistic, and grounded in everyday life
- Avoid corporate, staged, or overly polished scenes

## Composition
- Single moment in time; human context implied
- No symmetry-heavy setups or flat-lay shots
- Natural perspective at eye-level or contextual angles

## Lighting
- Natural or practical indoor lighting
- No dramatic studio lighting or cinematic treatment

## Subject matter
- Realistic environments and believable objects
- Avoid any ad-like or promotional composition
